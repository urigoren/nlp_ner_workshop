section {num} purpose of plan . 
the name of the plan is the patheon n . v . {num} omnibus incentive plan ( the " plan " ) . the purposes of the plan are to provide an incentive to selected officers , employees , non-employee directors , and consultants of the company or its affiliates ( as hereinafter defined ) whose contributions are essential to the growth and success of the business of the company and its affiliates , in order to align the long-term financial interests of such persons with those of the company's shareholders , strengthen the commitment of such persons to the company and its affiliates and attract and retain competent and dedicated persons whose efforts will result in the long-term growth and profitability of the company and its affiliates . to accomplish such purposes , the plan provides that the company may grant options , share appreciation rights , restricted shares , restricted share units , share bonuses , other share-based awards , cash awards or any combination of the foregoing .
